# Gallery photos (15 slots)

The Gallery page shows a fixed grid of 15 photos, 5 rows of 3, each a
4:3 rectangle. Drop your photos here, ideally already cropped close to
a 4:3 ratio so they fill each tile cleanly.

Name them anything; just update the matching entry's `src` in
`src/data/gallery.js` to point at your filename. There are exactly 15
entries in that file, one per grid slot.
